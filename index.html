Welcome to Fancy Pants Cafe!

We are an imaginary cafe in the heart of room 4.116 of Holmesglen Institute's Moorabbin campus. Born of the need to complete an assessment task we were thought into existence by Robert Warrandyte in an attempt to satisfy their demanding academic overlords completely reasonable expectations.

Since then we have grown into a comfy and cozy hideaway for all manner of folk from gnomes and elves to druids and warlocks, and everyone in-between, who find themselves seeking a respite from the daily grind.

Winter is coming... and so is our new menu!
